SAIC acts as a collaborative and symbiotic linkage between TIET and its Alumni. The organization functions with a vision to develop a dynamic student-alumni community committed to establishing connections and strengthening ties between students and alumni, facilitating TIET’s mission of providing top quality education and opportunities for investing in its future.

 

ABOUT US

 

Student Alumni Interaction Cell (SAIC) is an institute body run by the students of TIET, under the guidance of TIET Alumni Association & Dean of Student Affairs (DoSA), devoted towards the enhancement of relations between the students and alumni community.
